📊 Mini Project: Sales Dashboard Analysis (Power BI).

I recently worked on a sales dataset to uncover insights that can help improve business performance.

The Problem
Many businesses generate sales daily but struggle to understand:

✅Which days perform best.

✅What shipping methods customers prefer.

✅Which customer segment drives the most revenue.

Without this clarity, it’s hard to make informed decisions or grow effectively.

Key Findings
✅Sunday recorded the highest sales, while Tuesday had the lowest.

✅Standard Class shipping dominated both sales and number of orders.

✅The Consumer segment contributed the most revenue.

✅Sales showed steady growth, peaking in 2016.

Recommendations
✅Introduce promotions or discounts on low-performing days like Tuesday.

✅Focus more on the Consumer segment with targeted marketing.

✅Optimize Standard Class shipping since it’s the most preferred option.

✅Use historical growth trends to plan future sales strategies.

Conclusion
Data is more than numbers; it reveals opportunities for growth, efficiency, and smarter decision-making.

This project strengthened my ability to turn raw data into meaningful business insights that drive action.
